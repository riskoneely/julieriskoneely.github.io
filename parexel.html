Making clinical complexity navigable

Guide to Clinical Trials — Asia Pacific

Client

PAREXEL International

Role

Senior Designer — Publication Layout & Production

Scope

80-page publication, print & digital

An 80-page market guide for PAREXEL, a global clinical research organization, covering clinical trial landscapes across the Asia Pacific region. Work involved translating complex regulatory, statistical, and geographic data into clear, accessible layouts within PAREXEL's established brand standards — producing a publication quality enough to serve as a key sales and educational tool across 13 countries.
