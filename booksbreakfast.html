Twelve years. One community. One brand.

Brand Identity, Print, Events & Communications — Books & Breakfast

Books & Breakfast is a nonprofit serving at-risk children in Evanston with before-school programming, providing a morning meal, homework support, and a community where kids feel valued. In partnership with SocialRaise, I developed the brand identity including logo design and applied it across annual reports, direct mail campaigns, event invitations, fundraising materials and digital communications. The organization grew from five volunteers in one school to eight schools, serving hundreds of students through private funding.
