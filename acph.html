American Conference on Physician Health

Design Lead — Brand, Event, Campaign, Print, Digital — AMA / Mayo Clinic / Stanford Medicine
Coloring wall designed by Max Kalef.

The American Conference on Physician Health (ACPH) is a biennial event focused on physician well-being, sponsored collaboratively by the American Medical Association, Mayo Clinic and Stanford Medicine. I developed a design system for the event and applied it across all communication touchpoints including social media, print materials, event catalogues, signage and promotional banners. From concept to final execution, I handled every design need for the event, ensuring consistency, clear messaging, and a cohesive visual experience for attendees.
