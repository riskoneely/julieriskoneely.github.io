Women in Medicine Social Campaign

Client

American Medical Association

Role

Design Lead — Brand, Digital Campaign

Channels

Social Media, Digital Display, Web Banners

A social media campaign celebrating Women in Medicine Month — designed to honor AMA physician members with bold, portrait-driven social assets that demanded attention and communicated pride. Each execution was built around a consistent visual system flexible enough to feature dozens of individual physicians while remaining immediately recognizable as a campaign.
