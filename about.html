About

I'm an experienced graphic designer looking for a collaborative, curious team to call home. I love creating smart, meaningful work alongside talented people who work hard and don't take themselves too seriously. I care about brand, typography and beautifully made things, and I've spent my career turning marketing ideas into thoughtful, well-crafted design across print, digital and physical spaces.

When I step away from work I refuel in the peace and beauty of the wilderness, often with one of my sons or a friend along for the ride.
