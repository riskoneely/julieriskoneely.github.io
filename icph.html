International Conference on Physician Health

Design Lead — Brand, Event, Campaign, Print, Digital — AMA / BMA / Canadian Medical Association

The International Conference on Physician Health (ICPH) is a global conference focused on physician well-being, co-sponsored by the American Medical Association, the British Medical Association and the Canadian Medical Association. I developed a visual identity for the event and applied it across all communication touchpoints, ensuring consistency, clear messaging, and a cohesive visual experience.
