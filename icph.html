A global conversation, unified by design

International Conference on Physician Health

Client

AMA / BMA / Canadian Medical Association

Role

Design Lead — Brand, Event, Campaign, Print, Digital

Year

2022 — Orlando, Florida — 500 participants

Complete end-to-end conference identity for ICPH 2022, a global physician health conference co-sponsored by the AMA, the British Medical Association, and the Canadian Medical Association. The distinctive geometric pattern system and lime green palette created an immediately recognizable identity that scaled across environmental graphics, column wraps, retractable banners, stage backdrop, step-and-repeat, program book, and digital assets.
