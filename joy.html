When recognition becomes a campaign

Joy in Medicine — Integrated Campaign

Client

American Medical Association

Role

Design Lead — Brand, Campaign, Event, Print, Digital

Impact

14M+ impressions · Renewal of 3 major health system contracts

A national integrated campaign recognizing health systems committed to physician well-being. Work spanned out-of-home advertising, digital display, event signage, print collateral, and customized crystal awards personalized for each recognized health system. The campaign generated 14M+ impressions and directly supported renewal of three major health system contracts.
