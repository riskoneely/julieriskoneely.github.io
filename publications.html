Design in service of public health

AMA Publications & Educational Content

Client

American Medical Association

Role

Design Lead — Brand, Print, Digital

Scope

Annual reports, advocacy publications, 15+ physician toolkits

A body of work spanning annual reports, large-format advocacy publications, data-driven reports, and an extensive library of educational toolkits designed to help physicians navigate practice management, leadership development, racial equity, and emerging issues including AI in medicine. All content produced within a complex multi-step compliance and legal review process, translating high-stakes healthcare policy into clear, compelling visual narratives.
